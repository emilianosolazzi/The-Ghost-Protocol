# The-Ghost-Protocol 🥀⛓️

**"Memecoin by day. Truth registry by night."**

GhostProtocol is a dark-fun, on-chain GameFi memecoin experiment where users submit evidence, earn $GHOSTED rewards based on severity, and unlock story content through token-driven progression. Built with Solidity, React, and TypeScript, it blends verifiable on-chain actions with social gameplay and a bold cyber-noir interface.

### The Ghosting Receipt — Your On-Chain Proof of Pain

For 0.0095 ETH — about the price of a sad coffee — you mint a cryptographic proof of your humiliation.

You submit a hash of your evidence, a severity score from 1 to 100, and a drama type. The contract timestamps it forever. Nobody can edit it. Nobody can delete it. The blockchain remembers even when you wish it wouldn't.

- 30% of the fee goes to the Treasury.
- 70% stays in the protocol.
- If you're the direct victim (not a proxy), you earn $GHOSTED tokens immediately. Severity 100 pays 5,000 tokens. Severity 1 pays 50.

The contract knows the difference between a slow fade and a full disappearing act.

---

### The Proxy Witness

Not everyone submitting is the one who got ghosted. You can file on behalf of a friend — a proxy submission. The contract tracks this separately. Proxies get no token reward. They get only the quiet dignity of having done the right thing.

**Direct victims: rewarded.**  
**Proxy witnesses: respected.**  
The contract knows who bled.

---

### The Locked Story

Every piece of evidence becomes a Locked Story. The details are hidden. If you want to read what really happened, you have to pay.

There are three ways in:

- **Burn your way in**: Pay in $GHOSTED. Half is burned forever, half goes to the submitter.
- **Credibility unlock**: High credibility score holders read for free (and gain more score by doing so).
- **Direct ETH payment**: The submitter receives 100% — no burn.

Every unlock makes the next one more expensive. Demand proves value.

---

### The Ritual of Truth

Anyone can stake 100 $GHOSTED and declare: *I believe this story is real* — or — *I think this is fake*.

The oracle (trusted witness) resolves it. Get it right → 200 $GHOSTED back + credibility boost. Get it wrong → stake is split (half to submitter, half burned). Streaks are recorded on-chain.

---

### The Credibility Score

This is the soul of the protocol. It is not bought. It is earned.

It rises through correct truth assertions and responsible unlocks. A high score means the community trusts your submissions. You become a verified witness.

There is no shortcut. Credibility must be proven over time.

---

### The Burn

Every burn unlock and every wrong assertion destroys tokens. The more the protocol is used, the scarcer $GHOSTED becomes. Pain compounds into scarcity.

---

### The Submitter's Revenue

Your humiliation becomes a revenue-generating asset. Every unlock pays you — half from token burns, everything from direct ETH payments. The more people want your story, the more you earn.

---

### The Two-Step Power

Ownership transfers require two signatures (propose + accept). No accidental rugs. The oracle, treasury, and pause functionality are all upgradeable with safety valves.

---

### What the Contract Does Not Do

It does not read your messages. It does not verify emotional truth. It only holds the hash and records who staked on it.

The truth lives in the screenshots. The contract holds only the immutable proof.
